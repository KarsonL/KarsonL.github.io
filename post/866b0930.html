sqlzoo 练习3——SELECT from Nobel

发表于2021-07-27|mysqlsqlzoo
|阅读量:

该博文转载自我的CSDN博客： sqlzoo 练习3——SELECT from Nobel_Karson.L的博客-CSDN博客

sqlzoo-SELECT from Nobel Tutorial: https://sqlzoo.net/wiki/SELECT_from_Nobel_Tutorial

1、Change the query shown so that it displays Nobel prizes for 1950.


2、Show who won the 1962 prize for Literature.

3、Show the year and subject that ‘Albert Einstein’ won his prize.

4、Give the name of the ‘Peace’ winners since the year 2000, including 2000.

5、Show all details (yr, subject, winner) of the Literature prize winners for 1980 to 1989 inclusive.

6、Show all details of the presidential winners:

  • Theodore Roosevelt
  • Woodrow Wilson
  • Jimmy Carter
  • Barack Obama

7、Show the winners with first name John


8、Show the year, subject, and name of Physics winners for 1980 together with the Chemistry winners for 1984.

9、Show the year, subject, and name of winners for 1980 excluding Chemistry and Medicine

10、Show year, subject, and name of people who won a ‘Medicine’ prize in an early year (before 1910, not including 1910) together with winners of a ‘Literature’ prize in a later year (after 2004, including 2004)

11、Find all details of the prize won by PETER GRÜNBERG

那个非ASCII 的字符 Ü 应该是要特殊处理的（给了链接，进不去），但是直接写进去，答案又是可以运行的。

12、Find all details of the prize won by EUGENE O’NEILL

单引号不能直接用，用 两个单引号 表示一个单引号

13、List the winners, year and subject where the winner starts with Sir. Show the the most recent first, then by name order.

使用 ORDER BY 进行排序， 并通过关键字 “ACS(升序)” 和”DESC(降序)” 控制顺序。

14、The expression subject IN (‘Chemistry’,’Physics’) can be used as a value - it will be 0 or 1.
Show the 1984 winners and subject ordered by subject and winner name; but list Chemistry and Physics last.

解析： subject IN (‘Chemistry’,’Physics’) 是一个判断，故可视为一个bool值（0或1）。
展示1984年的winner和subject 并按照subject进行排序，但是将Chemistry and
Physics放在最后。也就是说，Chemistry and Physics 要特殊处理，先排完其他，再排Chemistry and
Physics。

使用 CASE… END 嵌套 WHEN…ELSE 来处理 “subject IN (‘Chemistry’,’Physics’)” 的问题。然后 ODER BY 对 subject 和 winner进行排序。
